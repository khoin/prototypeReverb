🤔 personal considerations (i.e., deviations from the paper) & misc.

  • i didn't employ linear interpolation nor all-pass interpolation anywhere in this implementation
  • 32 samples chosen for max excursion depth (because Dattorro's sample-rate is roughly half of 48 kHz)
  • decayRate is not T60. what? (this is an open question, but I believe it is possible to calculate)
  • decayDiffusion2 is free, instead of bounded to decayRate
  • refer to README.md for a write-up of these considerations and other minor details
